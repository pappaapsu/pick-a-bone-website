# pick-a-bone-website
Official website for Pick-a-Bone BBQ in Fenton, MO. A family-owned roadside legend for nearly 20 years. Specializing in 1.5" thick pork steaks, fall-off-the-bone ribs, massive turkey legs, and award-winning seasonal white chicken chili. Located at 104 Schneider Dr. by the Pink Elephant. Follow us for authentic, low &amp; slow smoked meats!
