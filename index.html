Our Story

For nearly 20 years, Pick-a-Bone BBQ has been a family-owned roadside legend in Fenton, Missouri. We pride ourselves on serving authentic, low & slow smoked meats that keep our community coming back generation after generation. From our famous 1.5-inch thick pork steaks to our fall-off-the-bone ribs, every dish is prepared with passion and tradition.
